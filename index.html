Research

I am broadly interested in robotic related topics (if you group such topics in Control, Planning/Learning, and Perception), as my academic background mixed mechanical, computer, and electrical engineering. That being said, my focus area is robotic perception, especially tactile sensing (multimode with vision as well) for enhancing robotic dexterity and control under contact-rich tasks.

Currently I'm working on developing a high-coverage, high-durability, and high-frequency tactile sensing skin for humanoid robotic hands, under the guidance of Prof. J. Edward Colgate at HAND ERC, Center for Robotics and Biosystems, Northwestern University.
