Research

I am generally interested in robotics related topics (I usually group them as control, learning/planning, and perception), as my academic background mixed mechanical, computer, and electrical engineering. That being said, my focus area is robotic perception, especially tactile sensing for enhancing robotic dexterity under contact-rich tasks. I believe multimodal perception with tactile information input will help us build safer human-robot interactions.

Currently I'm working on developing a high-coverage, high-durability, and high-frequency tactile sensing skin for humanoid robotic hands, under the guidance of Prof. J. Edward Colgate at HAND ERC, Center for Robotics and Biosystems, Northwestern University.
